Casa Mila




Casa Mila also known as La Pedrera meaning stone quarry is currently the headquarters of the Catalunya-La Pedrera Foundation. Public tours and cultural events are held and managed by the foundation. Built between 1906-1912 La Pedrera is regarded innovative due to the undulating stone facade, which is self supporting and self supporting columns and the underground parking garage.
